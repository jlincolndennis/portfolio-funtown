My name is Lincoln. I am a full stack web developer and/or writer from Denver, CO.

Additionally, I have a background in theater, education, and English (via a BFA from the University of Northern Colorado). I'm also really good at those claw machines filled with stuffed animals you often find in bowling alleys.

Experiencing it, analyzing it, over-analyzing it, and decorating my life with it. And occasionally, I contribute some of my own. I am particularly interested in the intersection between art and tech, and how they transform and enhance each other.

I also enjoy arguing, cats (specifically this one), and Doctor Who, which is probably why I spend so much time on the internet.
