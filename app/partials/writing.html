2015: The Year in Media

Media, art, pop culture — whatever you want to call it — is a profoundly important part of my life. When I close my eyes and try to remember what 2015 felt like, among images of my friends and family and experiences are Steven and Amethyst, Kate and Clint, Rey and Fin, Jupiter and Caine and more. And what’s most exciting of all, back on January 1st, 2015, I had no idea these some of these characters or the pieces of art they are from existed, or if I did, I didn’t know how they’d exist for me. And now its January 1st, 2016. What wonderful things exist outside of my current perception that I will get to experience in the coming year?
